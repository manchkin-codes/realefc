About Reale Hospital FC

Club History & Identity

Founded as a representation of Reale Hospital, our football club has evolved into a powerhouse in Kenya's Division 2. We embody the values of health, resilience, and community spirit both on and off the pitch.

Our journey from humble beginnings to topping Division 2 is a testament to our commitment to developing local talent and playing attractive, competitive football that brings pride to our community.

Our Vision

To establish ourselves as the premier football development club in Kenya, creating pathways for local talent to reach professional levels while maintaining our community roots and competitive edge.

Our Mission

To compete at the highest levels of Kenyan football while developing local talent, promoting health and fitness, and serving as a positive force in our community through sportsmanship and excellence.

Core Values

  • Integrity: Fair play on and off the pitch
  • Community: Serving and representing our people
  • Excellence: Striving for the highest standards
  • Development: Growing talent and character
  • Passion: Love for the beautiful game

Home Ground - Potters Academy

Location: Potters Academy Grounds, Kenya

Surface: Natural Grass Pitch

Capacity: 1,500 passionate supporters

Facilities: Modern changing rooms, training area, spectator seating

Our fortress at Potters Academy Grounds is where we build our home advantage. The intimate atmosphere with passionate supporters creates an electric environment that opponents fear and our players thrive in.
